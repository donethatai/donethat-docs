You can manage your subscription in stripe by following the "Manage Subscription" button in your (settings)[/settings] page.

By default, all subscriptions are individual and can be upgraded to a team subscription.

After subscribing, you will recieve an email three days before the trial ends as a reminder.

To earn referral bonuses, create your referral ocde in (settings)[./settings]. Your friends can use this code during checkout and both you and your friend will get a free month of DoneThat.
