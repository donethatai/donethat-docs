This is the profile your followers and team members see. By default it is private. You can change that in [social](/social).

You can edit your profile picture, name, handle, title, and social links by simply clicking on the respective elements in the profile overview.

In [summaries](/summaries) you can edit and make changes to your summaries. In [stats](/stats) you can see more stats.

Add a handle to your profile to get a sharable link, and add a name so that others can find you when looking for people to follow. You can set your profile visibility in [social](/social).

Your title is meant like a job title, anything that allows others to better understand who you are when looking for people to follow or checking out your profile.
