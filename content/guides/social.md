See activity of people you follow, your members of your teams and organization, and public profiles. If you see people you don't recognise here, those are probably public profiles.

Comment or like other's activity to encourage them or align on work. If you like or comment on their activity, they will receive a notification with their daily summary email.

Your own profile is set to private by default. This means that only people you accept as followers will be able to see your activity.

When setting your visibility to team, organization, or public, broader audiences can see your activity by default, for example for easier alignment or accountability.

If you also set up a tag in [Profile](/profile), you get. apublic URL to share if your profile is public. You can set if your profile should also be indexed on google in that case.

Want to share your activity outside of DoneThat? You can set up integrations with slack or email to autoamtically update your colleagues.

To integrate with slack, follow the flow in the More section on the [Social](/social) page. Then select the channel and cadence of posting there.
