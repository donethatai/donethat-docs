Tasks are automatically assigned to projects. By default, AI will add up to eight projects if you don't add these projects yourself. You can always drag-and-drop tasks to different projects if there were assigned wrong.

You can drag and drop projects on top of each other to merge projects. Careful with this, this cannot be undone.

A "private" project will not count towards your total hours worked in your stats.

A "confidential" project will autoamtically make all tasks invisible in that project but still count towarsd your stats.

Projects can be "private" and "confidential" at the same time.

You can assign projects to a portfolio to group them logically. This has to be done manually.

Projects shared by a team will appear in every team member's project overview and their tasks will automatically be assigned to them as they fit.
