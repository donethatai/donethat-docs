The organization servers for combining teams and for billing. Everybody can only be member of one organization but of many teams.

If you set a domain for your organization, people joining DoneThat with that domain will automatically be added to your organization.

You can access your invoices by clicking on "Manage Subscription". 

When people join or leave an organization, the seat billing automatically adjusts.

To invite people to your organization or your team, go to their settings pages and add their email to invite. If you cannot invite somebody, review your team visibility settings. Some are set to only allow members from within your organization.
